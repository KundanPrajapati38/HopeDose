Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to HopeDose.

What items can I donate?

We accept a wide range of items including clothing, shoes, toys, books, non-perishable food, household items, and more. All items should be clean, in good condition, and usable.

How do I arrange for item pickup?

After submitting your donation information through our website, our team will contact you within 48 hours to arrange a convenient pickup time. We offer pickup services for donations within a 20-mile radius of our center.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, HopeDose is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

Where do my donations go?

Your donations directly support individuals and families in need within our local community. We distribute items through our community centers, partner organizations, and direct assistance programs.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to donate anonymously. During the donation process, simply select the "Donate Anonymously" option, and your personal information will not be publicly displayed.
