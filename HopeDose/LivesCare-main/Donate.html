Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our programs and initiatives. We allocate funds based on current needs, with a focus on education, healthcare, nutrition, and emergency relief. We maintain transparency by publishing regular reports on how donations are utilized.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to HopeDose are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes. Our organization is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Absolutely! You can choose a specific cause or program when making your donation. If you have a particular project in mind that isn't listed, please contact us, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

How do I know my donation is making an impact?

We provide regular updates through our newsletter, social media, and annual reports. These updates include stories of individuals and communities benefiting from our programs, as well as statistical data on our impact. Donors also receive personalized impact reports.

Can I set up a recurring donation?

Yes, you can set up monthly recurring donations through our donation form. Recurring donations help us plan and sustain our programs over the long term. You can modify or cancel your recurring donation at any time.
