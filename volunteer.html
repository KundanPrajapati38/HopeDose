Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to volunteer?

The minimum age to volunteer is 16 years. Volunteers under 18 will need parental consent and may have limited roles depending on the activity.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by role. Some positions require as little as 4 hours per week, while others may need more. We're flexible and can work with your schedule.

Do I need any special skills to volunteer?

Most volunteer positions don't require special skills, just enthusiasm and dedication. For specialized roles like digital marketing or event coordination, relevant experience is helpful but not always necessary.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We offer digital volunteer positions that can be done remotely, including social media management, content creation, and virtual event coordination.
